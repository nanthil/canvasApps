Dynamic rotating 2d vectors around an origin

This app was my first graphical application. I had built UI with a number of different libraries and frameworks, but I had never built anything purely graphical purely with a graphical api.

My intent was to learn more about vectors and practice abit of trigonometry in my programming.

The vectors rotate at a random speed that errs towards a higher speed the longer the vector. This allowed me to see that I can calculate the length of a vector, and that the angle indeed controls the rate of rotation around the origin.

Use the buttons to pause rotation.
